Description:

"IP Subnetting" is a versatile application that functions both as a calculator and a comprehensive training tool for test preparation. This application is fully customizable, offering the choice of languages, including English, Russian, Romanian, and German, as well as Light & Dark & Default theme to suit your preferences.

In addition to its practical uses, "IP Subnetting" introduces a scoring system and tracks answer rates to make the learning experience engaging and motivating for users. You can even make your scores public to see where you rank on a chart among other users. And for those who value privacy, you can easily switch your profile to private mode.

Get ready to master IP subnetting while enjoying a personalized and interactive learning experience in your language of choice.

Story:

This application has its roots in the story of my first Python application with a GUI developed using KivyMD. I attempted to port that app to Android, but unfortunately, I didn't succeed due to the lack of documentation and the fact that the author no longer supports it.

After some time, I made the decision to learn Jetpack Compose, and one of the best ways to learn was by creating a real application. This application you see is the result of that journey.
