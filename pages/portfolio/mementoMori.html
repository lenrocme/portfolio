Description:

An informational board designed to help you stay motivated, maximize your time, and live life to the fullest. By displaying the difference between your current age and the average life expectancy, this board serves as a reminder of the preciousness of time and encourages you to take action and make the most of every day.

Memento Mori is a Latin phrase that means "remember that we are going to die one day." It is often used as a reminder to people to live their lives to the fullest and not take their time on earth for granted

Story:

Honestly, even though I published this application on Google Play like my other applications, I feel that I made this one primarily for myself. When I reflect on my past, I realize there were many times when I could have been more productive, not just in work but in various aspects of life. Did it help? No.
